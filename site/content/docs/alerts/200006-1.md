Sensitive data is anything that should not be accessible to unauthorized access, known as sensitive data. Sensitive data may include personally identifiable information (PII), such as Social Security numbers, financial information, or login credentials. Sensitive Data Exposure occurs when an organization unknowingly exposes sensitive data or when a security incident leads to the accidental or unlawful destruction, loss, alteration, or unauthorized disclosure of, or access to sensitive data.
